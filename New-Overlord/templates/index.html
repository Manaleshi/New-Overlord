Quick Start Guide

  1. Generate a World: Create the game map with terrain and settlements
  2. Lock the World: Click "Lock World for Game" when ready
  3. Start Your Faction: Choose a starting location and create your faction
  4. Submit Orders: Plan your moves, studies, and actions for the turn
  5. Process Turn: Watch as your orders execute over 30 game days
  6. Read Report: See what happened and plan your next move
